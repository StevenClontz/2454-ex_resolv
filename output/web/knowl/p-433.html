Sejam \(f\colon I\subseteq\R\to\R\text{,}\) contínua em \(L\in I\) e \(g\colon A\subseteq\R^r\to\R\text{,}\) tal que \(\lim\limits_{(x,y)\to(x_0,y_0)} g(x,y)=L\text{.}\) Então
\begin{equation*} \lim\limits_{(x,y)\to(x_0,y_0)}f\big(g(x,y)\big)=f\Big(\lim\limits_{(x,y)\to(x_0,y_0)}g(x,y)\Big)=f(L)\text{.} \end{equation*}
